# Data sources

This file summarizes the data used in the empirical analysis.

## Variables

- EURO STOXX 50: target market index.
- Brent Oil: oil price variable.
- Euro area spot rates at 5, 10 and 30 years: interest rate variables.
- EONIA and €STR: overnight interest rate variables.
- M2 and HICP: used to construct the real money supply variable.
- EER40: euro effective exchange rate index.
- VSTOXX: volatility index used to identify periods of high uncertainty.

The raw datasets are stored in `data/raw/`, while the processed datasets are stored in `data/processed/`.
